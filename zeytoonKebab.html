Our Story

Persian Flavour,
Aurora Nights

Zeytoon means "olive" in Persian — a symbol of warmth and generosity we bring to every table on Yonge Street. Guests consistently point to the same things: portions are generous, the owners are welcoming, and the space is always spotless.

"Tender, juicy chicken and flavourful, fluffy rice — nothing overly salted or too rich. We'll definitely be back."

From our appetizer platter with warm flatbread to our late-night menu for guests craving something after hours, Zeytoon Kebab is Aurora's home for honest, fire-grilled cooking.
